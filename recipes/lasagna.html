Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a great meal. My mother used to make it back in the good 'ol days. Try this recipe for a delicious trip down memory lane.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Layer ingredients in pan
  4. Cook in oven for 1h 30m at 375 degrees
    • Alternatively cook at 900 degrees for 5 minutes
