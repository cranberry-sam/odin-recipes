Ice Water

Description

There is nothing quite as refreshing as a glass of ice water. This recipe will show you how to prepare one in excruciating detail.

Ingredients

  • Water

Steps

  1. Gently pour some of the water into an ice cube tray.
  2. Place ice cube try into the freezer for 2-3 hours or until water is sufficiently frozen
  3. Remove the tray from the freezer. Twist the try side to side to break ice cubes out of the tray.
  4. Fill a glass, cup, or container with water, making sure to leave room for the ice.
  5. Gingerly place two to three ice cubes into the water.
  6. Wait 3 to 5 minutes until the ice cubes have sufficiently chilled the surrounding water
  7. Have a sip and enjoy!
